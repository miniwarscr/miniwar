## 🔧 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

### Mini War Script GUI does not appear after execution
- Make sure your executor is fully injected before pasting the Mini War Script
- Try re-executing the Mini War Script after waiting 5 seconds
- Restart the popular title and your executor, then try again

### Auto farm stops working mid-session
- Check your internet connection — the Mini War Script requires a stable connection
- Verify that the popular title has not been updated — download the latest Mini War Script version
- Lower the `speed_multiplier` in your configuration if your device is struggling

### Auto collect misses some items
- Increase the `collect_interval` to give the Mini War Script more time per cycle
- Make sure no other overlays or scripts are interfering with the Mini War Script

### Mini War Script crashes on mobile
- Free up RAM by closing background applications
- Ensure you are running the recommended OS version for full Mini War Script compatibility
- Reinstall the executor and re-paste the Mini War Script

### Executor fails to inject
- Run the executor as administrator (PC) or grant full permissions (mobile)
- Temporarily disable antivirus software — it may be blocking the executor
- Download a fresh copy of the executor from a trusted source
